About The Project

This series of videos highlights the impact of Title IX on girls and women in sports. By design, It's a diverse group. The youngest subject is a high school athlete, the oldest a 94-year-old great grandmother. The group includes a World Cup champion, a public-health official, a university president, a mayor, a Presidential press secretary, a children's author, a former professional sports league commissioner and many others.

Student reporters from the The Shirley Povich Center For Sports Journalism asked the same question of 60 women: "What’s the most important lesson you learned playing high school sports?"
